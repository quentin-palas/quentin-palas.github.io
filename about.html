Who are we ?

We are a French brand designed to improve the efficiency of an ordinary wallet. Thanks to Cartesafe, you will experience no hacking of credit cards or loss of your cards at any time. Our online card shop offers innovative and secure card holders, what better can we offer you? Passionate about minimalistic gadgets, we decided to share our passion through Cartesafe. Small, simple and convenient to use, our card holders guarantee you an effective security to avoid the theft of your banking information.
